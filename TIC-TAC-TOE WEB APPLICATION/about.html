The Story

Tic-Tac-Toe, also known as Noughts and Crosses, is one of the oldest and most beloved games in human history. Dating back to ancient Egypt, this simple yet strategic game has entertained millions across generations.

Our modern implementation brings this classic game to life with stunning visuals, smooth animations, and multiple game modes to keep you engaged for hours.

How to Play

1

Choose Your Symbol

Select whether you want to play as X or O (in 2-player mode, X always goes first).

2

Take Turns

Players alternate placing their symbols on the 3x3 grid.

3

Win the Game

Get three of your symbols in a row (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal) to win!

4

Avoid a Draw

If all 9 squares are filled without a winner, the game ends in a draw.
